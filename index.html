01 / about

I'm a security researcher focused on web exploitation, binary reverse engineering, and OSINT. Most of my public work comes out of competitive CTFs, where I write detailed, reproducible walkthroughs instead of just posting a flag.

Outside of competitions, I do independent vulnerability research and occasional bug bounty work, reporting responsibly and documenting root causes rather than just proof-of-concepts.

This site collects the writeups, tools, and notes I'd want to find myself when learning a new technique.
